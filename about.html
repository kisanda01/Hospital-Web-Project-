About Us

Discover who we are and why we care about your health.

Our Story

Care Compass Hospitals was founded with a vision to provide world-class healthcare services to all individuals. With three branches located in Kandy, Colombo, and Kurunegala, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional medical care combined with compassion and innovation.

Over the years, we have grown to become a trusted name in healthcare, offering comprehensive services including diagnostics, emergency care, and wellness programs. Our team of dedicated professionals is committed to your well-being and aims to set new benchmarks in the healthcare industry.

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, affordable, and reliable healthcare services, ensuring the well-being of our patients with the highest standards of medical excellence and personalized care.

Our Vision

To become the leading healthcare provider in Sri Lanka, known for innovation, compassion, and unparalleled quality in patient care.
